Copyright and Fair Use

WELCOME TO THE SCAVENGER HUNT

As a designer, understanding copyright and fair use is essential when creating content. This scavenger Hunt is designed to help instructional designers, educators, and anyone who creates content explore key concepts surrounding the legalities when utilizing creative resources when designing. Throughout this activity, you will read and navigate a variety of online resources focused on the concept of copyright and fair use. In order to do so you will find answers to questions in effort to deepen your understanding about how to use, create, and share educational resources responsibly.

About the American Kennel Club and Trick Titles

Welcome to your journey towards earingin American Kennel Club (AKC) trick titles with your dog! The AKC is a recognized authority in dog breeding, training, and competition, dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of dogs. One of their exciting initiatives to promote pet owners to do more with their dogs is the AKC Trick Dog program. This program encourages owners to teahc their pets various tricks, and in return reward them with titles showcasing their accomplishments.

Trick titles are a fun way to bond with your dog while enhancing their obedience and giving them mental stimulation. The program offers various levels of expertise, including Novice, Intermediate, and Advanced, each requiring the completion of specific tricks. Whether you're new to training or looking to refine your dogs skills, this site provides resources and guidance you need to achieve these titles together.

Getting Started

  • Explore the Tricks: navigate through the list of tricks for each title. Keep in mind you have to achieve these titles in order, starting from Novice and working your way to Elite Performer.

  • Track your Progress: Use the progress tracker to log the tricks you rdog has mastered. Celebrate each achievement as you work towards the next title!

  • Have Fun: Remember, the most important part of this journey is to enjoy spending time with your dog. Training should be a rewarding experince for both of you.
